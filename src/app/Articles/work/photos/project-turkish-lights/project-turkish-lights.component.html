Turkish lights

Back story:
This picture was taken back in 2020 at a traditional souk (market) in Djerba Midoun, while I was testing my new canon 24-70mm f/2.8L lens. The store sells traditional clothes and decoration pieces.

Below are some of the settings for Geeks:

ISO: 400 Focal ratio: F/2.8 Shutter speed: 1/250 Focal length: 70mm

This work is a showcase for a common situation which is, you can take a good picture but sometimes it has some elements that you wish you could take off.

Work done

Color correction and grading: Restoring the shadows and highlights, color grading the picture and adding depth to the picture.

Photo manipulation: Removing the price ticket.
Masking and repainting lost informations, such as the pants behind the ticket.
