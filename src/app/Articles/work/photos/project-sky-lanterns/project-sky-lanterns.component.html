Sky lanterns

Back story:
This was shot back in 2016 in very poor conditions during a social event at Beb Souika, Tunis.
Below are some of the settings for Geeks:

ISO: 3200 Focal ratio: F/4.5 Shutter speed: 1/15 Focal length: 30mm

This picture was shot with a Canon 60D and a kit lens, the white balance was off, the noise was high and it was late to capture the moment (the lanterns launch). So here I tried to fix what could be fixed

Work done

Color correction and grading: Restoring the shadows and highlights, color grading the picture and adding life to the picture.

White balance correction: Balancing out the warm colors, so white colors doesn't appear orangey.

Noise reduction: Removing the noise due to the high ISO, thus smoother colors and cleaner picture.

Adding back some of the sky lanterns: Since I was busy filming the lanterns launch, by the time this picture was taken, I missed most of it in the background, so I had to compose it back.
