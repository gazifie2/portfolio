Back story:
I used to stay up at night working, so sometimes I go to events without sleeping. As a result, I had puffy eyes In this shot.
Below are some of the settings for Geeks:

This picture is a showcase for fixing puffy eyes, and cleaning skin.

Work done

Color correction: Correcting colors to restore the natural skin color and make the face pop a little more in the picture since the color of the skin is so close to the color of the umbrella behind.

Skin retouching: The skin looks rough, so it required some touch-ups and some "skin care".
Remove spots and pimples, and retain the natural skin look. We don't want to over do it for men.

Fixing puffy eyes: Puffy eyes can make you look tired or less attractive, that's definitely my case, so I fixed that.
There are many techniques to do it, for me I find cloning and repainting is the best mathode that works for me.
